Solve Notion's Google Docs Import Limitations

Notion's Import Problems

  • Single page per document: Multi-tab Google Docs become one Notion page
  • No bulk import: Can't import multiple Google Docs at once
  • Header structure lost: Long documents don't split into manageable pages
  • No folder support: Must import documents individually

How Docs2Notion Solves This

  • Converting Google Drive folders into organized Notion workspaces
  • Splitting multi-tab documents into separate pages
  • Creating individual pages from document headers
  • Preserving formatting, tables, and document structure
  • Enabling bulk migration from Google Workspace to Notion

Perfect for teams migrating documentation, SOPs, project files, knowledge bases, and structured content from Google Docs to Notion while maintaining organization and accessibility.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Docs2Notion is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Google Authentication: When you sign in, we temporarily access your Google account to read the documents you specify
  • Document Processing: We process your Google Docs content in your browser - content never leaves your device
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how our service is used (page views, conversion rates)

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and convert your Google Docs to Notion format
